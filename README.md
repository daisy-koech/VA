## Portfolio highlights

### Notion VA Command Center
A complete virtual assistant operating system built in Notion which includes a home dashboard, task tracker, client CRM, Automation hub, appointments tracker, and SOP library. Built to demonstrate systems thinking and operational organisation for online business owners.

### 30-day social media content calendar
A structured content calendar built in Notion and Google Sheets covering post topics, captions, hashtag sets, platform assignment, and scheduling slots across a full month.

### Client management database
A relational Notion CRM for tracking client details, communication history, project status, and follow-up reminders — replacing scattered spreadsheets with one clean system.

### Social media graphics pack
A set of branded social media templates designed in Canva: Instagram posts, story templates, quote cards, and promotional banners with consistent colour palette and typography.
